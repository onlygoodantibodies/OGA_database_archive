About Us

Only Good Antibodies (OGA) Community

The OGA community is a diverse collaboration of basic, clinical, data, and behavioural scientists and technicians from academia, industry, and non-profit organisations, led from the University of Leicester. We are part of the wider movement to improve research integrity and reproducibility, and we are an affiliate stakeholder in the UK Reproducibility Network. We are learning from this wider community, and our work also represents an opportunity for learning in the wider context of research reproducibility and integrity.

Our Collaboration with YCharOS Inc.

The Only Good Antibodies community and YCharOS Inc. work together to:

  • Increase the availability and use of well-performing research antibodies
  • Eliminate the use of inappropriate antibodies and the associated waste of animals for their production
  • Promote education and awareness about best practices in antibody use and validation

Open Science Partnership

We are an open science partnership with 26 major science stakeholders in institutions, commercial manufacturing, reagent databases, research funding, and publishers. Come to us for help finding the most robust antibodies for your experimental needs.
